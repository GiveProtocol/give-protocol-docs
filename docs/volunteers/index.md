## Why Volunteer Through Give Protocol?

### Verified Service History
Your volunteer hours are recorded on the blockchain, creating a tamper-proof record of your service. Perfect for job applications, school requirements, or personal tracking.

### Skills-Based Matching
Our matching system connects you with opportunities that fit your specific skills and interests, whether you're a developer, designer, teacher, or anything else.

### Flexible Commitment
Find opportunities that fit your schedule, from one-time events to ongoing commitments. Filter by remote or in-person options.

### Recognition & Rewards
Earn badges and credentials that showcase your commitment to service. Some organizations offer additional rewards for their volunteers.

## Getting Started as a Volunteer

1. **Create your profile** - Add your skills, interests, and availability
2. **Browse opportunities** - Search for positions that match your criteria
3. **Apply** - Submit applications to organizations you're interested in
4. **Get matched** - Organizations review applications and accept volunteers
5. **Track your hours** - Log your service time as you volunteer
6. **Earn credentials** - Build your verified volunteer portfolio

[Create your volunteer profile](/docs/getting-started/creating-account/)

## Types of Opportunities

- **In-Person Volunteering** - Local opportunities in your community
- **Remote Volunteering** - Skills-based work you can do from anywhere
- **Event-Based** - One-time or short-term commitments
- **Ongoing Positions** - Regular, recurring volunteer roles
- **Skills-Based** - Opportunities leveraging specific expertise
