## Why Join Give Protocol?

### Reach Crypto Donors
Access a growing community of cryptocurrency holders who want to support charitable causes. Many crypto donors specifically seek out blockchain-based giving platforms.

### No Setup Fees
There are no fees to join Give Protocol or get verified. You only pay a small percentage when you receive donations.

### Transparent Accountability
Build trust with donors through blockchain-verified transactions. Every donation is publicly verifiable, demonstrating your organization's transparency.

### Global Reach
Accept donations from anywhere in the world without the complications of international wire transfers or currency conversion.

## Verification Requirements

To get verified on Give Protocol, organizations must:

1. Be a registered 501(c)(3) or equivalent nonprofit
2. Provide valid organizational documentation
3. Complete KYC (Know Your Customer) requirements
4. Pass compliance review

The verification process typically takes 3-5 business days.

[Start the verification process](/docs/organizations/verification/)

## Fee Structure

- **No setup fees** - Free to join and get verified
- **1% protocol fee** - Applied to received donations
- **Gas fees** - Standard Moonbeam blockchain transaction fees
